Useful Sites For Travel

  1. tabc.io - service of instantaneous comparison of prices for railway tickets, air, buses, tours and even blablakar. To find the cheapest way to get to a place, use it. In one place you can compare the price of an air ticket, a train, a bus and even a blablacar.


  2. tripadvisor.ru - one of the largest tourist sites in the world. The main usefulness of the service is the travelers' opinions about everything in the world: cities, places of interest, hotels, restaurants, excursions and so on.


  3. aviasales.ru - service for the search and booking of cheap air tickets. But for your own confidence, you can double-check the directions in other search engines.


  4. ecolines.net - international bus lines that carry messages between the 21st country and 205 cities in Europe. It is the largest and oldest carrier of Eastern Europe. For independent European trips Ecolines fits perfectly; flights are very inexpensive, and buses are comfortable.


  5. mcruises.ru - a site where you can choose and book a sea or river cruise around the world. Service unites all the world's largest and Russian cruise companies, announces special offers and discounts.


  6. economybookings.com - the site is the leader of Runet for car rental abroad. Booking is possible in more than 800 companies that are engaged in car rentals in 150 countries. 20 thousand auto-rental points, Russian-language support service, low prices and high quality of services made this service the absolute leader of the market.


  7. blablacar.ru - another world leader. A service uniting drivers and fellow travelers around the world. Just specify the city of departure, arrival and date to see the list of drivers who offer trips along your route, and choose your own. Before contacting, you can see the driver's profile with a photo, brief information about yourself and reviews.


  8. booking.com - the well-known site, which presents ads from individuals - the owners of apartments and houses.


  9. tutu.ru - search engine for air and railway tickets in Russia. It is convenient in the first place for a complete and up-to-date schedule of commuter trains.


  10. maps.me - free application for phones on any platforms. We use only these cards, and they are just beautiful! After installing the application, you need to download the map of the country you need, and then a map with all the signs, sights, hotels, hostels, restaurants will work offline! If your phone supports GPS, then when combined with maps.me, you can forget about the awkward communication on the French / Chinese / Portuguese curve in trying to figure out exactly where to get off the bus, and how to find a museum.


  11. translate.google.ru - Google Translate as an Android or iPhone application will help you find a common language with foreigners without any problems. In offline mode, more than 90 languages are supported, though it is necessary to download the necessary language packs in advance. Us this translator saved not once, but, certainly, translation of phrases is far from always ideal.
